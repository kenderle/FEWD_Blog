The Horror

One day I was at home, taking cat naps, chasing sun spots, enjoying my usual things. Hoping for maybe a fly to come in when the human did, but not hoping too much.
When the human did come home, I felt the happy-sad I usually did. Happy at the prospect of an overflowing food bowl and sad at giving up my premium couch spot.



Unfortunately, it was an even worse day than that. The human had torture in mind.

"DOOOORRRRRIIIAAAANNNNN! Here, kitty, kitty!"



Cats are naturally clean creatures you see, which is part of the reason I decided to make this blog.
There is no reason for the indignity that is routinely forced upon me and I ask my brethren to rise up and END THIS OPPRESSIVE PRACTICE OF FELINE HYGENIENE SUBVERSION!!!
